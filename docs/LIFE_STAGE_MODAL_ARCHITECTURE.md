**LIFE_STAGE_MODAL_ARCHITECTURE.md**  
## **1. MODE: [JUVENILE_HIGH] // THE CATCHY START**  
* **Target:** Younger audience, high energy, the "first win."  
* **Lingo:** High-density Tsotsi Taal, fast-paced, rhythmic.  
* **Vibe:** Brash, confident, catchy hooks that "stuck" in the head.  
* **Theme:** The dream, the first 4+1 ride, the excitement of the "vaya."  
## **2. MODE: [ADOLESCENT_NOIR] // THE INTIMATE STRUGGLE**  
* **Target:** Emotional depth, mid-level growth, the "dark night of the soul."  
* **Lingo:** Moody, slow-burn R&B, heavy double-entendres about love and loss.  
* **Vibe:** Depressive but beautiful. The pressure of life, the heartbreak of the grind, the "Dark R&B" texture.  
* **Theme:** Isolation in the mountains, the "itch" of addiction/obsession, the internal war.  
## **3. MODE: [MATURITY_ZEN] // THE MANIFESTATION**  
* **Target:** Self-realization, purity, the global stage.  
* **Lingo:** Clean, focused, high-frequency English mixed with "Grootman" wisdom.  
* **Vibe:** Romantic but stable. Peaceful, visionary, Hebrews 11:1 realized.  
* **Theme:** Purity of the heart, the "Blueprint," marrying the craft, global awareness.
